Rufus Du Sol

Rufus Du Sol is my favorite band. They are an electronic music group from Sydney, Australia and they have been making music since 2010. I picked them for my web page because I listen to them all the time (and they're performing at Folsom Field in August!).

The members

  • Tyrone Lindqvist - vocals and guitar
  • Jon George - keyboards
  • James Hunt - drums

Their albums

  1. Atlas (2013)
  2. Bloom (2016)
  3. Solace (2018)
  4. Surrender (2021)

Some songs I like

If you have never heard of them, here are a few songs to check out:

  • Innerbloom
  • Alive
  • No Place

You can find out more about them on their website.
